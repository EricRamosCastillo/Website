Project 1

Cyclistic Bike Share

Release Date: October 2021

Summary:

In this capstone project, I answer the marketing director's question of how casual users and annual members differ and make recommendations to the marketing department based on my findings. The process involved cleaning 12 months worth of data, performing exploratory analysis, and creating business recommendations to convert casual users into annual members.
